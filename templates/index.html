About our club:

The Melbourne Univerisity Psychology Association is an University of Melbourne Student Union affiliated club that provides social networking opportunities and academic support. We have social events such as Board Games Night, Movie Night, BBQs for our members to mingle and form connections. Our flagship events are the Peer Assisted Learning Seminars, which is are revision sessions for core psychology subjects like MBB1, Biological Psych etc. These sessions has helped students for their exams with practise questions, brief lecture overviews and peer discussions.

Join us!

Register as a member in person at any of our events, or use our QPay link to sign up online. Membership is $5 and lasts until the end of the year.
